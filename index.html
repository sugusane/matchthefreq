How It Works
Lives
Each player starts with a set number of lives. Win a set, and the other side loses one life.
Rounds
Each set is made of several rounds. Every round gives you a target sound to match by ear.
Rules
Listen during the preview, then guess the frequency before the timer ends. Faster and closer guesses win the round.
Win Condition
The player who keeps more lives after the set finishes wins the match.

Privacy Policy

This game stores only gameplay data needed for active rooms (username, room code, settings, and round guesses).

No payment data is collected. Room data is removed after players leave or the room is closed.

By playing, you agree to fair-use limits that protect the game from spam and abuse.
